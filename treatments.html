01 / What we do
Four core methods

The tools, and when each one fits.

Chiropractic

Hands-on assessment and care of joints, movement and mechanical dysfunction — adjustment, mobilisation and graded exercise.

Acupuncture

Used selectively where it adds value alongside hands-on care — particularly for muscle tension, persistent pain and headaches.

Soft-tissue work

Targeted release for tight, irritable tissue — helping joints move freely and pain settle more reliably.

Movement & self-help

Practical exercise, posture and lifestyle advice — what you do between sessions is what makes the difference stick.

02 / What a visit looks like
Session by session

From first contact to feeling better.

01
First visit · ~45 min

Assessment & honest plan.

Full case history, physical examination, clear explanation of findings, and a tailored plan — including whether further investigation makes more sense.

02
Follow-ups · 20–30 min

Hands-on treatment & review.

The right mix of chiropractic, soft-tissue and (where appropriate) acupuncture, with a clear sense of progress at every visit.

03
Between visits

Practical home advice.

A small, manageable set of exercises and movement guidance — tailored, not generic. Most progress happens here.

04
Maintenance (optional)

Stay well.

Some patients return periodically once recovered, others don't. There's no pressure either way — only what genuinely helps.
