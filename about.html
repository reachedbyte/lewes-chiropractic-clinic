01 / The practitioner

Meet Helle Henriksen, DC, CCSP.

Helle graduated from the Anglo-European College of Chiropractic in Bournemouth in 1987. After five years in Essex she moved to Lewes in 1992, and later completed postgraduate certification as a Chiropractic Sports Physician.

"My approach is thorough and unhurried. I want patients to leave understanding what's going on — and confident in the plan to address it."
Helle Henriksen, DC, CCSP
