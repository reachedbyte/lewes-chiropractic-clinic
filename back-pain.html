01 · Back Pain

Back pain that finally makes sense.

Lower back pain, stiffness, postural strain and restricted movement — assessed properly, explained clearly, and treated with care that fits your life.

01 / How care helps

What we typically see in clinic

We begin with a careful conversation and physical assessment. The aim is to understand what's actually driving the issue — not just where it hurts.

  • Lower back pain & stiffness
  • Postural & lifting strain
  • Sciatica-type leg referral
  • Recurring "going" backs
  • Disc-related discomfort
  • Pain after a long sit or drive
02 / Common symptoms

Does this sound familiar?

  • Aching across the lower back
  • Sharp pain bending or twisting
  • Stiffness on waking
  • Discomfort referring to a leg or buttock
  • Difficulty sitting or standing too long
03 / What care looks like

Considered, hands-on, evidence-informed

  • Spinal mobilisation & adjustment
  • Soft-tissue & trigger-point release
  • Targeted exercise & loading
  • Posture, sleep & lifting advice
