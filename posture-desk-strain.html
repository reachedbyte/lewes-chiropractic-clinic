08 · Desk & Posture Strain

Long hours at a desk shouldn't cost you.

Hours of seated work add up — neck, upper-back, jaw and shoulder strain are the most common results. Care looks at how you sit and how you move, not just where it aches.

Book your visit → Call 01273 483327
01 / How care helps

What we typically see in clinic

We begin with a careful conversation and physical assessment. The aim is to understand what's actually driving the issue — not just where it hurts.

  • Neck & upper-back tension
  • "Tech neck" headaches
  • Forearm & wrist strain
  • Hip & low-back stiffness from sitting
  • Jaw clenching contributors
02 / Common symptoms

Does this sound familiar?

  • Tight neck & shoulder by mid-afternoon
  • Headaches building through the day
  • Aching upper-back between shoulder blades
  • Stiffness standing up after sitting
  • Discomfort in forearms or wrists
03 / What care looks like

Considered, hands-on, evidence-informed

  • Mobilisation & release work
  • Postural retraining
  • Workstation & screen advice
  • Movement breaks & home exercises
